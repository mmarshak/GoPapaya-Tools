FAQ

 

Why would / should I use this app?

We all have different preferences as for what, where and when to eat, but planning ahead isnt always possible. With GoPapaya you can check at the very last minute from a selection of close-by dining options, get an instant discount on your meal and be seated on the spot. For once, someone is going to reward you for being spontaneous. Go ahead and start experiencing restaurants that up until now may have been outside your price range and/or comfort zone. GoPapaya guarantees to give you more value for the price you pay.

 

When should I expect to see deals?

GoPapaya shows real time availability of deals for immediate seating, deals will be displayed only if it currently available. You should expect to find deals for restaurants in the area during restaurant's regular opening hours, assuming the restaurants provide incentives at that time. Open the app when you are ready to eat. Please do not expect to see deals for lunch if this is 9AM in the morning :)

 

Does it cost money?

No, it is free to get promotions for the restaurants and you do not need to pay ahead for the promotion or the food.

 

How does it work?

When you use GoPapaya you can make your selection from a list of restaurants that are in the area with indication what is the deal/promotion and what is the table type availability. Each restaurant will offer you a promotion on your meal as long as youre able to check in within a given timeframe set based on your location (it is typically 30 minutes).

The promotion may expire any moment and also the amount of tables offers for each promotion is limited. Therefore, do not wait too much time before making your mind, be spontaneous.

When you are ready to go to the restaurant and you are in the area (typically less than 5 miles away) of the restaurant you can get the instant deal and a table reservation with just two taps. Pick the group size 1, 2, 3 or 4 people, and click the Claim the Deal button. After you get the immediate confirmation on the app, you have a table on hold for you for the next 30 minutes.

Remember that when you click Claim the Deal the clock starts ticking. Once you walk in to the restaurant open the app to make sure you are automatically check-in in the app.  After that just check in with the host to be seated and get the amazing promotion and food. You do not need to show your mobile device to the host. Please remember to identify to the host that you made the reservation using GoPapaya.

 

How do I get the discount?
Upon checking in, your host will be able to identify you by name and seat you and your guest/s. After you enjoy your meal and are ready for the check, youll notice that the applicable discount rate has been applied to your bill. Simply pay with your preferred method of payment and youre good to go. Dont forget to spread the word with your friends.

 

What about tipping?

Please remember to tip your server on the total bill (prior to discount). Thanks!

 

Can I claim the restaurants deal after I am already in the restaurant?

The promotion available through the app is only for guests that claim the deal before they arrive to the restaurant, the restaurant will not honor deals that were claimed after the guest is already seated.

 

What should I do if the app does not show that I am checked in?

Make sure you have internet connection and the Bluetooth is enabled. If this does not help, approach the host and show him/her your confirmation email or SMS text message.

 

We are a party of more than 4 people, can we use the app?

The app supports today deals for party of 4 or less, if you are in a bigger group you can divide the group to few smaller sub-groups where each one is 4 people or less, and each smaller sub-group needs to claim the deal separately.  However, the restaurant cannot guarantee that all the sub-groups will seat together.

 

Will people around me know that I am getting a discount? Will I feel embarrassed when I get seated?

No, they wont. You do not need to show any coupon or your mobile device to the host. You need just to identify yourself with your name and that you made the reservation with GoPapaya. How much you pay and the discount you may receive is unknown to other guests at the restaurant.  The restaurant will apply the applicable discount to your bill. No further action is required from your end.

 

How would I get notified about the status of my order?

After you clicked Claim the Deal we will notify you with the various stages of your order. You will get update on the mobile device (by alerts or notifications), by email and if also provided your mobile number, we will send you SMS text messages.

The various type of updates you will get are:

   Confirmation that the table is secured and waiting for you.

   When you checked-in by the app upon your arrival to the restaurant.

   Your cancelation of the order.

 

What happens if Im unable to check in on time and I still want to get the promotion?

You could still try to get to the restaurant ASAP but we will no longer be able to guarantee that youll receive the promotion or have a table. When you arrive to the restaurant check with the app if the promotion is still on and try to Claim the Deal again and check in right away, you may still enjoy the discount offered after all. Make sure to claim the deal prior entering the restaurant, otherwise the deal will not be honored by the restaurant.

 

What happens if I change my mind after claiming the deal?

The amount of tables offer for each promotion is limited. Therefore, in order to be fair with other people we ask you to go to the app and cancel the order as soon as possible.

 

What happens if I claim a deal and continuously do not show up to the restaurants?

The app is free to claim new promotions, and we really want to keep it that way. If you miss many times showing up to the restaurant it hurts GoPapaya reputation with the restaurant as well it prevents others to get the promotions, the number of promotion per restaurant is limited.

So please try to be nice. We reserve the right to remove users who do not play nice.

 

How would I be notified on new promotions nearby?

The best way to know about new promotions is just to open the app when you want to eat out, and you will get the latest list of offering that are available now for you.

If you give the app permission to send you notification, then we may also send you notifications about special promotion. Rest assured, you will only receive those promotions that are most relevant to you based on your preferences.  Please keep in mind that you can always go to the phones app setting and turn the notification either on or off.

But again the best ways to find out about the active promotions is by opening your GoPapaya app when you want to eat out.

 

How can I get SMS text update for my active orders?

You can enter/delete your mobile device number in the Account setting in the app (it is on the navigation bar on the left). If you delete the mobile device, you will not get any more SMS text updates. If you still want to keep the phone number listed in the app, you can still unsubscribe, text STOP to 857-228-6826. Or if you want to re-subscribe, text START to 857-228-6826 (if you deleted the phone number from the app you will need to reenter it again). If you need assistance, text HELP to 857-228-6826. Please note that standard data and message rates may apply for text message alerts. Please contact your mobile phone carrier for details.

 

What if I want to make a reservation for a different day?

We do understand that sometimes advanced reservation is necessary. GoPapaya is for those times when you did not plan, but are still interested in dining out. We are working very hard to ensure youll be able to enjoy a great meal for a great price and we can only make it happen when youre just about ready eat. When you make advanced reservation you end up paying regular price, therefore we are not offering advanced reservation services at this point.

 

Can I pay with the app for the restaurant after finish my fine meal?

At this time, we do not offer payment through the app.

 

I have ideas how to make the app better, do you care to know?

Of course we will love to know how we can make GoPapaya better for you, please send us an email at suggestions@gopapaya.mobi.

 

Why discount rates between restaurants may vary?

The owner / manager of each restaurant has the ability to set their own discount rate depending on availability and occupancy which fluctuate over time.

 

How can I list my restaurant on the app?

You can use the same app and your existing GoPapaya account both to manage your restaurant promotions as well as use it for your own benefit to get promotions to restaurants. In order to join the GoPapaya restaurant networks you are required to sign an offline engagement letter with GoPapaya. After that you will need just to logout and login to the account and you will be able to enjoy both worlds: get promotions to other restaurant and also control promotions to your own restaurant, how cool is that J

How do you choose the restaurants you are working with?

GoPapaya only works with high quality establishments and will not send you to a place that we would not recommend to a friend. You will always receive an actual list of restaurants in close proximity, that are able to offer you a unique promotion at a particular moment and a table.

 

What are you going to do with my sign up information?

GoPapaya takes your privacy very seriously, we do not share and/or sell your personal information to third parties. The only information received by the restaurant is your first and last name, in order to facilitate the reservation process. GoPapaya use you email account to update you with your orders and notify you of future offerings. In the case you provide a mobile number, we will use it to update you with yours orders.

For more details please review GoPapayas privacy policy and terms of services documents.
