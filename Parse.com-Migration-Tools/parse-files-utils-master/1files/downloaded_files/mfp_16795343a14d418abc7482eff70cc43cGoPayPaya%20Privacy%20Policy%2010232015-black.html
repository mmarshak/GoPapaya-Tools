1. What Types of Information Do We Collect From You?

Personally Identifiable Information (or "PII") ") is information that identifies you as an individual. In order for you to take advantage of particular opportunities provided through the Sites (for example, to use the services offered on the Sites), we may require that you furnish PII. We collect your PII, such as your name, postal address, telephone number, e-mail address, credit card number or other payment account number (including the three (3) or four (4) digit validation code for your credit card), date of birth, when you voluntarily provide it to us or when your employer provides any such PII to us.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information (or "Non-PII") is aggregated information, demographic information and any other information that does not reveal your specific identity. We may collect your Non-PII, including information about the device you are using in accessing and/or using the Sites, information from referring websites, and your interaction with the Sites. We also may aggregate PII in a manner such that the end-product does not personally identify you or any other user of the Sites, for example, by using PII to calculate the percentage of our users who have a particular telephone area code. For example, GPS information that we receive from your mobile device may be provided to restaurants in an aggregated and/ or anonymous form to help develop offers and limited deals to end-users.

Cookies and Other Technology A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent by GoPapaya to your Internet browser and stored on your computer's hard drive or it may be stored in your mobile device, and that delivers information about you and your activity on the Sites. Pixel tags and web beacons are small graphic files that can function in various ways (such as allowing us to track how you view an email that we send you, and track your activity on the Sites) and are commonly used in conjunction with cookies. We and other third parties detailed below under "How Do We Use Information Collected From You?" may use cookies, pixel tags, web beacons, and other similar technologies to better serve you with more tailored information and facilitate your ongoing use of the Sites. If you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, there is a simple procedure in most browsers that allows you to decline the use of cookies. To learn more about cookies, please visit the third party site http://www.allaboutcookies.org/. Some browsers allow you to indicate that you would not like your online activities tracked, using Do Not Track indicators (DNT Indicators), however we are not obligated to respond to these indicators. Presently we are not set up to respond to DNT Indicators. This means that latent information about your online activities may be used by us to improve your use of our Sites, but such usage is consistent with the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

IP Addresses are the Internet Protocol addresses of the computers that you are using. Your IP Address is automatically assigned to the computer that you are using by your Internet Service Provider (ISP). Your IP Address is identified and logged automatically in our server log files whenever you visit the Sites, along with the time(s) of your visit(s) and the page(s) of the Sites that you visited. Collecting IP Addresses is standard practice on the Internet and is done automatically by many web sites.

Data Collected from Beacons. Through the course of providing our Service, we may collect information from an end users device any time the end users app comes within range of a beacon (a small device that uses Bluetooth technology to identify our app and may be placed at the front of the house of our restaurant partners). GoPapaya relies on an app-specific identifier that is used to identify a unique app user. This identifier is associated only with the specific Customer app and is not tied to your mobile device. If you delete the Customer app from your device, this identifier will be deleted as well. Relying on this app-specific ID, we collect your location (latitude/longitude coordinates) and the time of day and your location when your device is near any of our beacons. We also collect device information, such as the device type, model and operating system version, and current IP address. We use this information to identify when you enter or leave the range of a particular beacon or the location where we have a beacon in order to provide analytic tools to help our restaurant partners better serve and understand their end users. We never collect personally identifying information such as a name, mobile phone number, email address, or other contact information from our Customers end users using our beacon. If you do not want the GoPapaya beacon to identify your mobile device, you can: (a) delete the Customer app or adjust the in-app settings of the app if the app makes that option available, or (b) turn off your Bluetooth through your devices Settings feature.

Data Collected from GPS. 

Whenever you use the Services, GoPapaya will collect detailed location information, for example in the form of GPS signals and other information sent by your mobile device on which the App is installed and activated.  GoPapaya uses your location and your location history while using the App to send targeted offers to you based on your current location and your location history.  You can disable location services for any application on your mobile device but it will limit your ability to receive offers/promotions based on your current location.

iTunes App Store.  GoPapaya uses iTunes to make iPhone, iPod and iPad apps available for download through the Apple's App Store. You do not need to register or provide personal information to view the GoPapaya app in the App Store. If you use the iTunes application to view information about GoPapaya apps, iTunes collects information specified in its privacy policy.

If you have an iTunes account and download an app, you may rate or review the app. If you review a GoPapaya app, your personally identifiable information will be visible to other iTunes visitors, including GoPapaya staff. The amount of visible personal information will depend on your account settings. To avoid displaying your personally identifiable information, you should not create an iTunes account, download apps, or post ratings.

GoPapaya does not collect, maintain, disclose, or share any information about people who download GoPapaya apps. Apple collects and provides non-identifying information on how many times an app is downloaded and the continent it is downloaded from. This information is password protected and only available to GoPapaya managers and the administrators of the GoPapaya iTunes apps, who require this information to perform their duties. The iTunes privacy policy is available at: http://www.apple.com/privacy/.
